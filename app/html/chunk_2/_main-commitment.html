OUR COMMITMENT

Our Commitment is best-in-class service and corporate citizenship through continuous improvement and expansion of our network. We offer quality solutions that assist our customers in achieving their objectives safely and efficiently by utilizing our quality resources, technology, and conservation.

COST EFFECTIVE

We commit to a cost effective approach by diversifying our operations and deliverables to save costs, improve performance, and reduce risk while ensuring the best value for any stakeholders freight investment. Cost diversification guarantees all freight is delivered on time, in good condition, and at a competitive rate. LC regularly evaluates our performance and competitive advantage on the market.

TECHNOLOGICALLY ADVANCED

We commit to constant innovation and prioritize technological advancement by always planning ahead. IT Teams carefully analyze emerging technology to understand its full potential and seek opportunities to collaborate with partners that are front runners in technology. Our technological portfolio includes digital connectivity, freight management, end-to-end visibility, and highest security standards.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY

We commit to sustainable practices and reducing our carbon footprint by applying energy efficient measures not only saving our costs, but contributing to saving our environment as well. LC always seeks optimized routes that will increase fuel efficiency, recycle tires and cartons, have regular scheduled truck and trailer maintenance to reduce carbon emissions, and strive to become a paperless company.

CORPORATE CITIZENSHIP

We commit to corporate citizenship in every community we operate in, and we invest in wellness and education programs to support our customers career growth and overall well-being. We value equity, diversity, and inclusion. LC has always committed to conducting our business in a highly ethical and conscious manner. Our continued commitment to our corporate citizenship results in improving our actions regarding sustainability, community involvement, employee well-being, governance, and ethics.
